About the Game

All Otome is a unique dating sim experience focused on realism and the art of letter writing. Enjoy classic features like branching storylines, multiple endings, and immersive choices, but with a twist: every romance route is a different version of you.

Features

  • Realistic, branching conversations
  • Handwritten-style letter exchanges
  • Multiple endings based on your choices
  • Rich, character-driven storytelling
